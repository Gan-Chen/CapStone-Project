Bee Excinction

Bees have been around for 110-140 million years now and in this new environment, they start to lose their grip on surviving from the hundreds of pesticide to the lack of habitats for living, and most importantly dangerous parasites that kills them. Not only that but they pollinate over a third of our food supply and 90% of wild plants. A disapperance of them would certainly leave a huge impact on our whole planet!
